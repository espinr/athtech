AthTech21 will also run alongside a number of atheltics meeting in the region.

  • Mon 20th Sep - Zadar High Jump Challenge
  • Tues 21st Sep - Athletic League Zadar 2021
  • Wed 22nd Sep - Zadar SepT&Fest EAP Meeting
  • Thurs 23rd Sep - Zadar University Games T&F Match

Something about being able to test your tech out, compete yourself and watch high quiality athletics.

Mon 20th Sep - Zadar High Jump Challenge

Top level street high jump show to take place on the evening of Monday 20th September on the spectacular "sea organ" in Zadar old town center.

There will be lighting, music and wall displays, as well as jumpers capable of over 2.20m (men) and 1.90m (women).

Tues 21st Sep - Alpe-Adria Athletic League Zadar 2021

U18 match between Croatia, Italy and Bosnia & Herzegovina on Tuesday 21st September.

Wed 22nd Sep - Zadar SepT&Fest EAP Meeting

International meeting from EAP series, with top Croatian able-bodied and paralympic athletes and their peers from around Europe.

Thurs 23rd Sep - Zadar University Games T&F Match

Track and field match as part of Zadar University Games (which will be held the same week in Zadar - also including futsal, basketball and volleyball tournaments).

Anyone attending the AthTech21 conference will also be able to compete if interested.
